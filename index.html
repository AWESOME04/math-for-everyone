Empowering Minds Through Mathematics

At Math for Everyone, we believe that mathematical literacy is the foundation for success in our increasingly data-driven world. Our mission is to make math accessible, engaging, and empowering for learners of all ages and backgrounds.

Join Our Mission

About Us

Founded in 2015 by a group of passionate mathematicians and educators, Math for Everyone is dedicated to breaking down barriers in mathematical education. Our team of experts works tirelessly to develop innovative programs and resources that make math approachable and exciting for everyone, from young children to adults returning to education.

Our Programs

Math Mentorship

Connecting students with math professionals for personalized guidance and support.

Community Workshops

Free math workshops in underserved communities, making math accessible to all.

Online Learning Platform

Interactive online courses and resources for self-paced math learning.

Math Clubs

After-school programs fostering a love for math through fun activities and challenges.

Our Impact

Since our founding, we've made a significant difference in mathematical education:

  • Over 50,000 students reached through our programs
  • More than 1,000 volunteer mentors engaged
  • Partnerships with 200+ schools across the country
  • 90% of participants report increased confidence in math

Success Story: Alex's Journey

"Math always scared me, but the mentorship program at Math for Everyone changed everything. Now, I'm pursuing a degree in Data Science and using my skills to make a difference in my community." - Alex, Program Participant
